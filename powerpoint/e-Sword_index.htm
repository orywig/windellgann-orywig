Topic e-Sword Modules

Scholarship of the King James Version (ver 8)   /     Scholarship of the King James Version  (ver 9)

There are many good features about the King James Version of which  the average reader is not aware.  This e-Sword booklet points out a number of these scholarly features.

Revelation  (ver 8)   /   Revelation   (ver 9)

This book module on "The Book of Revelation" provides some of the abundant evidence for the "early date" of Revelation and notes and outline of the book.  (Much of this material is also freely available in PowerPoint hyper-linked presentations from wgann@hiwaay.net if you wish to request it.)

Topical References  (ver 8)  /  Topical References  (ver. 9)

This is topical subjects which give linked scriptures to many basic Christian doctrines.  Easy and quick to located the places in the Bible that deals with these subjects.

Topical Studies - Gann  (ver 8)  /  Topical Studies - Gann  (ver 9)

This book in e-Sword topic format deals with a variety of subjects interesting to the Christian, such as "Capital Punishment", "Homosexuality", "Authority of the Scriptures" and several more.  All with detailed scriptural links that are quick and easy to read in e-Sword.

Walking Through the Bible  (ver 8)  /  Walking Through the Bible  (ver 9)

These are introductions to the books of the Bible and sometimes along with a sermon outline from that book.  This is the same material as that found on my web site "Walking Through The Bible"

The Church of Christ  (ver 8)  /  The Church of Christ  (ver 9)

This is a topical study on the identity, organization, and worship of the church of Christ as found in detailed linked scriptures.

Fundamental Bible Studies  (ver 8)  /  Fundamental Bible Studies  (ver 9)

This lesson series focus on fundamental topics such as God, Christ, the Holy Spirit, the Church, etc., in 25 lessons.  Handout lessons sheets are also available in PDF format.  (We used this e-Sword module in a college age class room setting with computers teaching how to use e-Sword to do one's personal research into the Holy Scriptures.
