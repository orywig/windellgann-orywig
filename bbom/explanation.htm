Explanation of the Program

 

HOW YOU MAY USE IT!

  



Information about the WordPerfect formatted files.



IMPORTANT! -- See the QUESTION at the bottom of this page!






  I had already been impressed with the advice given in Halley's Bible Handbook  on the title page, his statement  "The Most Important Page in This Book Is 814"*  [The page number may vary is different editions.] 


 Then while I was a student in a Bible college one of my professors told about a program of preaching  that he strongly recommended.  So this program is not
original with me.


The plan as I developed it called for studying from a book of the Bible for a month, usually in the mid-week Bible class, and then preaching from that book at least once on Sunday. 

  


    I alternate each month between studying an Old Testament book and a New Testament book.
    

I post a "reading sheet" and encourage everyone in the congregation to read the "Bible Book of the Month" and sign-up!  This is emphasized and encouraged so much that practically everyone in the congregation  participates.
    

The  effect is tremendous!  The congregation is reading the book!  They are studying from it in the mid-week Bible study, and they get at least one sermon from it on the Lord's Day!
    

I personally followed the program for many years, and have repeated many of the books. 

  


    Details:  I study one of the gospels every six months.  I also divide a few of the books into "Parts", such as Genesis Part 1  (ch. 1-11)  and Part 2 (12-50)  and Acts Part 1 (ch. 1-12) and Part 2(ch. 13-28) and the
  Psalms in 5 Parts according to the Book divisions.
    

   


    
      

 Some of the lessons  posted can be viewed on screen  or you may download a zipped  WordPerfect  formatted file.  These files are the lessons sheets I use as handouts each week.  You should print them out using the print Options, Booklet Printing.  The zipped file contains the full lesson-set for the book, including the Teacher's Notes when there are any. 
    



  Most of the lesson sheets in the future will be posted in the 
  Adobe Acrobat  PDF format, and you can download free the 
  Adobe Acrobat Reader needed for viewing and printing these lessons.  There are many advantages to this format and it is quite popular now.  The printed sheets will be ready to serve as a 'master' for making copies for your study group.
