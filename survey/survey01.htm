SELECT CORRECT ANSWER(S)

_____1. The world was created: 1. by God; 2. full grown; 3. by evolution.

_____2. The lights of the firmament were to be for: (1:14) 1. signs; 2. seasons; 3. days; 4. years.

_____3. God created man in: 1. the image of apes; 2. the image of angels; 3. his own image.

_____4. God told man: 1. be fruitful; 2. multiply; 3. replenish the earth; 4. subdue it; 5. have dominion.

_____5. God saw that everything he had made was: 1. so-so; 2. bad; 3. sickly; 4. good.

_____6. The Lord formed man from: 1. the air; 2. dust of the ground; 3. an evolutionary process; 4. fish.

_____7. A man shall: 1. leave father; 2. leave mother; 3. cleave to his wife.

_____8. The forbidden fruit: 1. looked good for food; 2. looked good to the eyes; 3. was desired to make one wise.

_____9. Adam called his wife Eve because: 1. she was a good wife; 2. she had made him sin; 3. she was the mother of all living.

____10. Lamech had: (Ch. 4) 1. two wives; 2. slain two men; 3. only two sons.
