SELECT CORRECT ANSWER(S)
 


_____1.  The Southern Kingdom was composed of: 1. Dan; 2. Judah; 3. Gad; 4. Benjamin. 



_____2.  Jeroboam's son Nadab (I Ki. 15:25-32) : 1. was king after him; 2. reigned fifty years; 3. was assassinated.


_____3.  The most wicked king of Israel was: 1. Isaiah; 2. Jeroboam; 3. Hezekiah: 4. Ahab.


_____4.  The contest on Mt. Carmel was between Jehovah and: 1. the church; 2. Baal; 3. Elijah.


_____5.  Jezebel (2 Ki. 9) in the end: 1. was thrown from a window; 2. rand down by a chariot; 3. eaten by dogs.



_____6.  Elisha's miracles included: 1. Jordan divided (2 Kings 2); 2. mocking children eaten by bears (ch. 2); 3. a girl raised from the dead (ch. 4)



_____7.  Elisha's miracles included: 1. Naaman healed of leprosy (ch.5) 2. causing a cow to fly (ch. 6); 3. causing iron to float (ch. 6).



_____8.  Israel was captured by: 1. Egypt; 2. Assyria; 3. Rome.



_____9.  Judah was captured by: 1. Medo-Persians; 2. Assyria; 3. Rome; 4. Babylon.



____10.  Israel was to: 1. be captive 149 years; 2. never return; 3. return mightily. but Judah was: 1. to remain free; 2. be captive for 70 years, 3. to never return from captivity.
