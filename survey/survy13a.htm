When Events Happened
6 BC Birth of Jesus ( some say between 6BC - 4BC)

6 AD Annas becomes high priest, Judea placed under procurators

14 Tiberius becomes the Roman emperor

26 Ministry of John the Baptist; Pilate made procurator of Judea

27 Jesus's ministry begins

30 Jesus crucified, resurrected, ascended; Church established

32? Stephen martyred

33? Saul converted (36 AD Pilate removed from office)

40 Cornelius and Gentile household converted

41 Claudius become Emperor; Agrippa I

41 Activities of church at Antioch (Acts 11& 12) James murdered

46 Paul's first missionary journey

48 Gathering at Jerusalem; second missionary tour (48 - 51/2)

51 Gallio becomes procurator of Achaia

52 Third missionary journey; Felix made procurator of Judea

54 Nero becomes Roman emperor

57 Paul arrested at Jerusalem, imprisoned at Caesarea (57-59)

59 Festus made procurator of Judea; Paul appeals to Caesar

59 Paul's voyage to Rome, under house arrest at Rome (60-62)

65? Paul killed at Rome; Jewish revolt against Rome (66 -73)

67? Peter killed at Rome

70 Jerusalem and temple destroyed by the Romans
