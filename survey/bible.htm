This Bible Survey course contains thirteen lessons -- plus maps and supplements to survey the Bible.
The lessons help the student to memorize this time line. All future study one makes of the Bible will add flesh to this skeleton outline of the Bible.

Periods of Bible History 



1.	PERIOD BEFORE THE FLOOD -- Genesis 1-5  (?? - ?? BC)  Adam
			WPD	PDF
			1. Creation; 2. Garden of Eden; 3. Adam & Eve; 4. Sin; 5. 1st Civilization

2.	PERIOD OF THE FLOOD -- Genesis 6-11  ( ?? BC - 2000 BC)  Noah
			WPD	PDF
			1. Wickedness; 2. Warning; 3. The Ark; 4. Ham, Shem & Jappeth;  
			5. Mt. Ararat; 6. Babel (languages)

3.	CHOSEN FAMILY -- Genesis 12-46  (2000 BC - 1800 BC)  Abraham
			WPD	PDF
			1. Abram is called;  2. Promises made;  3. Isaac;  4. Jacob

4.	EGYPTIAN BONDAGE -- Gen.47 - Exo.14 (1800 BC - 1440 BC) Joseph
			WPD	PDF
			1. Move to Egypt;  2. Enslaved;  3. Cry for deliverance

5.	WILDERNESS WANDERING -- Ex.15 - Deut. (1440 - 1400 BC) Moses
			WPD	PDF
			1. Deliverance, Red Sea; 2. Aaron; 3. Sinai; 4. Mosaic Law; 
			5. Tabernacle

6.	CONQUEST OF CANAAN -- Joshua (c.1400 BC)  Joshua
			WPD	PDF
			1. Gilgal; 2. Jericho; 3.Ai; 4. Conquest Campaigns; 
			5. Tribes receive land

7.	JUDGES -- Judges & Ruth  (1400 BC - 1043 BC)  15 Judges
			WPD	PDF
			1. Othniel; 2. Deborah; 3. Gideon; 4. Samson; 5. Samuel

8.	UNITED KINGDOM -- 1 Samuel, 2 Samuel - 1 Kings 11 (1043-931 BC)
			WPD	PDF
			1. Saul;  2. David;  3. Solomon;  
			4. Israel is a powerful rich nation

9.	DIVIDED KINGDOM -- I Kings 12 - 2 Kings 15  (931 - 721 BC)
			WPD	PDF
	1.	Northern kingdom is called Israel (10 tribes) 
		Jeroboam is first king
		a.	Each kingdom had about 20 kings, though Judah's
			 were spread over a longer period of time.
		b.	Some were: Jeroboam; Baasha; Ahab; Jehu
		c.	Israel was very wicked; taken captive by Assyrians 
			in 721 BC and never a separate nation again.  
			Rejoined Judah in 536 BC.

	2.	Southern kingdom called Judah (2 tribes) 
		Rehoboam was first king.
		a.	Asa; Jeohshaphat; Jehoash; Uzziah; Hezekiah; Manasseh

	3.	Prophets: Elijah; Elisha; Jonah; Amos; Hosea; Isaiah; Micah

10. 	JUDAH ALONE AND CAPTIVITY -- 2 Kings 15-25  (721 - 536 BC)
			WPD	PDF
	1. Kings: Hezekiah, Josiah -- Prophets: Jeremiah, Daniel, Ezekiel

	2.	Judah captured by Babylon and captives taken away in three waves.
		605 BC  (1) Daniel & 3 friends taken (associated with government)
		597 BC  (2) Ezekiel & others taken (lived in colonies)
		586 BC  (3) Temple and Jerusalem completely destroyed

11.	RESTORATION -- (Return From Exile) -- Ezra- Nehemiah- Esther (536-400 BC)
			WPD	PDF
	1.	Persia conquers Babylon and allows a re-nationalization of captives

	2.	Zerubabel rebuilds the Temple; Ezra restores the law; Nehemiah 
		rebuilds the walls.  Prophets: Haggai, Zechariah, Malachi.

	REVIEW of Lessons 1-11
			WPD	PDF

-- BIBLE HISTORY IS SILENT FOR 400 YEARS --  (Period between OT & NT)
	Time of Alexander the Great; Grecian Empire; beginning of Roman Empire

12.	LIFE OF CHRIST -- Matt. Mark Luke John (4 BC-AD 30) John & Jesus
			WPD	PDF

13.	CHRISTIAN AGE --  Acts thru Revelation  (AD 30 -- ??? )

	Christian Age - Part 1  Acts 1 - 13
			WPD	PDF
	Christian Age - Part 2  Acts 14 - 28
			WPD	PDF

	1.	Church established and grows.  Spreads through 
		Judah, Samaria, and uttermost parts of the world.    

	2. 	Peter & Paul
