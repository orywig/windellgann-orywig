SELECT CORRECT ANSWER(S)

_____1. There is no record of Abraham building an altar at: 1. Shechem; 2. Bethel; 3. In Egypt. (12:6-8)

_____2. Abraham feared for his life in Egypt because of: 1. his gold and silver; 2. his great herds; 3. his beautiful wife.

_____3. Melchizedek was: 1. king of Salem; 2. a priest of God, 3. received tithes from Abraham. (14:18-20)

_____4. God would not destroy Sodom if Abraham could find only; 1. fifty; 2. forty; 3. twenty; 4. ten; righteous persons there.

_____5. Abraham buried Sarah in: 1. the river Tigress; 2. the cave of Makepelah; 3. in Egypt. (23)

_____6. The servant of Abraham picked: 1. Ruth; 2. Ester; 3. Rebekah; for the wife of Isaac.

_____7. In Genesis 26:1-5; 1. The Lord appeared to Isaac; 2. A promise is renewed; 3. the Lord blesses Isaac; 4. Abraham's obedience is doubted

_____8. Esau: 1. was younger the Jacob; 2. a cunning hunter; 3. loved more by his father; 4. despised his birthright. (Genesis 25:24-34)

_____9. God's promise to Jacob in Gen. 28:13-15 included: 1. his seed would be as dust of the earth; 2. he would be great in territory; 3. in his seed all nations of earth would be blessed.

____10. Joseph was: 1. the son of Esau; 2. the son of Rachel; 3. despised by his brothers; 4. always had it good; 5. became great in Egypt.
